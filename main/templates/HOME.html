Welcome to the Social Media Handler

Use this tool to predict the engagement on your posts!

Whether you want to predict likes on your tweets, generate new tweets, or both, we've got you covered!

To use the predict likes feature, we will need your username, company inferred, day and time of posting, content and any media files that may be relevant.

To generate a tweet, we will need the topic you want to tweet about, company inferred, type of tweet, and the content.
